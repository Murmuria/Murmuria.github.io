Dan Fleck is currently a research professor in Computer Science at George Mason University (GMU). Dr. Fleck is a veteran of government contracting, teaching, and research. As part of GMU's Center for Assurance Research and Engineering (CARE) he has lead multiple GMU research programs encompassing network intrusion detection, malware analysis and network attack resilience. He also teaches a variety of courses at GMU including computer programming, software engineering, computer security and others.

In industry he spent over 10 years at SRA International culminating in being the technical lead for their R & D division. He has been the technical lead and project manager for successful DARPA programs including TIGR and Transformative Apps (TransApps). As an industry leader, he led teams from 5 to 50 people over the past 15 years. Throughout his career he cultivated an environment of constant process improvement and fun.
